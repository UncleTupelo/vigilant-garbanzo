pub fn module_owner() -> Option<T::AccountId>[src]

Optional pallet owner.

Pallet owner has a right to halt all pallet operations and then resume it. If it is None, then there are no direct ways to halt/resume pallet operations, but other runtime methods may still be used to do that (i.e. democracy::referendum to update halt flag directly or call the halt_operations).
